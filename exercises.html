Exercises

Cardiovascular Exercise

Also known as aerobic exercise, this type of exercise increases your heart rate and helps improve cardiovascular health. Examples include running, jogging, swimming, cycling, dancing, and brisk walking.

Flexibility

Flexibility exercises improve your range of motion and help prevent injuries. Activities like yoga, Pilates, and static stretching can enhance flexibility.

Sport

Participating in sports like soccer, basketball, tennis, or any other team or individual sport is a fun way to stay active and engage in exercise.
